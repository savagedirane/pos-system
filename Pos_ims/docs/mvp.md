MVP Definition & Roadmap
=======================

MVP Scope (Core features)
- User login with roles (Admin, Manager, Cashier)
- Product & category management
- Inventory stock tracking (stock levels, adjustments)
- POS checkout flow (cart, checkout, receipt)
- Sales recording and automatic stock deduction
- Daily sales summary

Short Roadmap
- Phase 1: Foundation (auth, core models)
- Phase 2: Inventory (stock movements, suppliers)
- Phase 3: POS Flow (cart, payments)
- Phase 4: Reporting & UX polish

Success Criteria
- Staff can complete sales in under 30 seconds
- Inventory updates correctly per sale
- System handles concurrent users reliably
